About Us

We are the first Forum Only full time clan family. The Alliance consists of four clans, three of which make up our war, and the fourth serving as a chilled, non-war farming atmosphere.

The Forum Alliance was formed by a group of forum members who felt out of place in other clans. They decided to come together around one thing that they all had a passion for- The Forums.

What we offer

As a Forum- Exclusive clan family, we have many benefits and offerings that far surpass anything that can be offered by a non- forum clan. Our Forum- Only membership allows us to give more trust and choice to members.
Listed below are just a few of the great benefits you will get from joining us:

  • Forum-only member to avoid global trolls!
  • An alliance wide 'funday' every other week- chat with others, play games, and visit other alliance clans!
  • War drop outs and reservations!
  • Effective three- clan feeder and educational system helps to take clashers from a new player to an experienced all- round clashomaniac.
  • An alternative to the feeder system, Forum Soaring offers a no- war, non- intensive environment for farmers!
